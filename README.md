# 🍽️ FoodLink
### AI-Powered Smart Food Waste Tracker for Communities

> 🥈 2nd Runner-Up | International FSM Hackathon 4.0

FoodLink is an AI-powered **Waste-to-Worth** ecosystem designed to bridge the gap between food donors and NGOs through an intelligent, centralized food redistribution platform. The system combines AI, geospatial intelligence, ESG analytics, food safety compliance, and smart logistics to minimize food wastage while maximizing social and environmental impact. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

---

# 🌍 Problem Statement

Millions of tons of edible food are wasted every year while thousands of people continue to suffer from food insecurity.

Current food donation systems suffer from:

- Lack of centralized coordination
- Delayed NGO communication
- Food spoilage due to manual logistics
- Poor transparency
- Limited recovery mechanisms
- Environmental degradation caused by landfill waste

FoodLink solves these challenges using AI-driven automation and intelligent decision making. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

---

## 🧠 Smart Priority Routing Engine

Instead of assigning NGOs randomly, FoodLink computes a **Priority Score** using:

- Expiry Time
- Distance
- Food Quantity

This enables:

- Urgency based ranking
- Smart pickup scheduling
- Logistics optimization
- Fuel-efficient routing

---

# 🔄 Complete Workflow

## Step 1

Individuals, restaurants and institutions upload surplus food.

↓

## Step 2

Food undergoes:

- FSSAI validation
- Expiry verification
- Image verification

↓

## Step 3

Priority Engine calculates:

Priority Score =

- Expiry
- Distance
- Quantity

↓

## Step 4

Nearest NGOs receive instant notifications.

↓

## Step 5

NGO claims pickup.

↓

## Step 6

Optimized route generated.

↓

## Step 7

Pickup completed.

↓

## Step 8

ESG dashboard automatically updates:

- Meals served
- CO₂ reduced
- Food diverted
- Carbon savings

This creates a complete closed-loop food redistribution ecosystem. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}
